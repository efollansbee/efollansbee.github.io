Searching for Orphaned Oil & Gas Wells

As a Research Scientist at Los Alamos National Laboratory, I conduct in situ and remote sensing of methane emissions from orphaned and abandoned oil and gas wells in the United States as part of the Department of Energy's CATALOG project.

Remote Sensing of Permafrost Melt

Poker Flats Research Range, Fairbanks, Alaska

I participated in field work to the Poker Flats Research Range near Fairbanks, Alaska to use drone mounted lidar to measure permafrost melt due to climate change.
